📚 Farming Playbook

Best practices for successful farming

🌾 Kharif Season (June-October)

  • Best Crops: Cotton, Soybean, Maize, Paddy
  • Soil Prep: Heavy ploughing required - 3 passes recommended
  • Irrigation: Depends on rainfall - 4-6 irrigations typical
  • Fertilizer: NPK 60:40:40 kg/hectare base dose
  • Pest Watch: Bollworms, stem borers, leaf folders

🌾 Rabi Season (October-March)

  • Best Crops: Wheat, Chickpea, Mustard, Lentil
  • Soil Prep: Light ploughing - 2 passes sufficient
  • Irrigation: 4-5 irrigations (winter saves water)
  • Fertilizer: NPK 50:35:0 kg/hectare - less potassium needed
  • Pest Watch: Aphids, cutworms, shoot fly

🌾 Summer Season (March-May)

  • Best Crops: Watermelon, Muskmelon, Groundnut
  • Soil Prep: Normal ploughing - 2 passes
  • Irrigation: Frequent - 6-8 irrigations during peak heat
  • Fertilizer: NPK 40:40:40 kg/hectare - balanced nutrients
  • Water Mgmt: Use drip irrigation to save 40% water

✅ Golden Rules for Higher Yield

1. Soil Testing

Test soil every 2 years before each major season. Know your NPK levels exactly.

2. Water Management

Use soil moisture sensors. Drip irrigation saves 40-50% water vs. flood irrigation.

3. Crop Rotation

Never grow same crop 2 seasons running. Rotate: Cereal → Legume → Oil crop.

4. Pest Prevention

Scout crops weekly. Control pests at early stage to save 30% chemical costs.

5. Record Keeping

Maintain farm diary. Track inputs, outputs, weather. Use data for next year planning.

6. Timely Operations

Prepare land 2 weeks before sowing. Plant during optimal window for that crop.
